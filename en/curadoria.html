Origin

Curation

The project emerges between Sao Paulo and Mantova, within a field of attention directed toward what remains culturally whole even under the pressure of commercial simplification.

Context

Sao Paulo offers a living, expanded, and constantly translated Italian-Brazilian experience. Mantova represents a reference of measure, form, memory, and Italian continuity.

Sigillo Italiano arises from the productive friction between these two layers: a culture preserved at its origin and a culture reinterpreted in displacement.

Curatorial method

The curation works through slow attention, contextual research, and refusal of excess. Rather than accumulating examples, it seeks to formulate a few recognitions with clarity, substance, and permanence.
